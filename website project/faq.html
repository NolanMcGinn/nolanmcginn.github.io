Frequently asked questions

Is this legal?

Yes this is 100% legal and we get all the permits needed for you to do this.

How do we keep the sharks alive?

There are a few ways like:

  • Feeding them fish you can buy or fish for.
  • Give them roadkill/Animals you legally hunted.
  • Or we can work out a deal and come feed them for you everyday.

How long can the sharks live for?

The sharks can live up to 30+ years if you are taking care of them and feeding them!

How do we get onto our property once the moat is built?

We install a draw bridge that is remote activated so only you or anyone you would like to come onto the property can come.

How long will it take to complete?

We can have it fully done within a month or 2 depending on how big you would like it to be and how many sharks you would like.

How do you get these sharks?

These sharks have been bread at our facility to be hyper intelligent and aggressive. No need to worry about them attacking you as we will let them be around and get to know you incase anything bad happens and you fall into the moat. But be careful with guests as they wont know them.
